} How Cancellation Policies Work

Registration Types: Policies can be configured differently for paid vs. free registrations, and for regular participants vs. organizers.

Cutoff Time: Cancellations are only allowed before the specified number of days and hours before the event starts.

Refunds: For paid registrations, you can choose whether to refund transaction fees (charged by payment processor) and application fees (platform fees) back to the user.

Policy Inheritance: Registration options can either use these tenant defaults or define their own specific policies.
